Get to Know Me

About Me

Creative Animator & Designer
Passionate Storyteller

Bringing Stories to Life for Little Minds 🌟

Hello! I'm Danyal Amin, a professional 2D Animator and Motion Graphics Designer with over 6 years of experience. My core passion lies in crafting cute, engaging, and educational animated videos that children love to watch, sing along with, and learn from.

Over the years, I've had the pleasure of partnering with leading kids' YouTube channels, educational institutions, and global publishers. Whether it's animating playful nursery rhymes like Twinkle Twinkle Little Star, rigging expressive animal characters, or composing vibrant motion graphics, I ensure every single frame is filled with magic, positive energy, and clear educational value.
